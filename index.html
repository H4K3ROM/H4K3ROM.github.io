II. Flash
a. Backup your existing Data (or not, just don't complain if you screw up your phone) TWRP 3.2.3 (https://twrp.me/oneplus/oneplusthree.html)
b. Factory reset if coming from another ROM (data, system & cache) Do NOT dirty flash over another ROM, start clean unless dirty flashing over THIS build
c. Flash the ROM .zip in revovery (don't flash anything else extra yet)
d. When the ROM flash completes, stay in recovery and now flash Gapps & Magisk (if desired for root)
e. Reboot & nJoy the Hackery!
III. Tips, tricks & "Under the Hood"
a. #Issue 1 - (after flashing newer builds, mobile data may need to be restored [only on generic carriers, this happens with Ting confirmed]
Easy fix = GoTo Settings/Network & Internet/Mobile network - (turn off Mobile data switch 5 seconds, then back on) - Your mobile service is restored
